I Have Moved - My New Blog

May 9th, 2013

For sometime I have been meaning to make a move to another blog host, or even write my own blog site. Well, atlast, I have done something about it. I decided to use Octopress as my blog ‘engine’ and GitHub Pages as the host.

The thought of writing my own blog from the ground up sounded kind of appealing, but that would take some time and effort and I would probably not learn a huge amount in the process. I would rather dedicate that time to learning something that really interests me, like writing my own Routing Slip implementation.

So, I thought Octopress / GitHub Pages was a good half-way house; I have more control over the site than most traditional blog sites, I am using GitHub every day to write my posts, I feel like a hacker but I am not having to write and maintain all the low level stuff had I written the site from scratch.

This blog will also give me the chance to “start over” - I want to blog more often. Hopefully this will give me some inspiration and I can make smaller, more frequent additions.

My next task is to migrate the content from TypePad to here.
